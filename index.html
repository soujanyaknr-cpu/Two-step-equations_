Key Vocabulary

Review each word before we begin. Say each one out loud!

⚖️
Equation
A mathematical statement showing two expressions are equal, using an = sign.
🔤
Variable
A letter (like x or y) that represents an unknown number we're solving for.
🔄
Inverse Operation
The opposite math operation — addition undoes subtraction, multiplication undoes division.
🎯
Isolate the Variable
Get the variable alone on one side of the equation by itself.
💎
Solution
The value of the variable that makes the equation true.

Introduction & Prior Knowledge

⏱ 3–4 min

Let's warm up with a one-step equation you already know how to solve:

🤔 Teacher asks: "What is the variable in this equation? What operation is happening to x?"
x − 9 = 28start here
x − 9 + 9 = 28 + 9undo subtraction → add 9
x = 37✓ solution
💬 Teacher says: "Great! That was a one-step equation. Today we will solve two-step equations, which require two inverse operations."
2

Teacher Modeling

⏱ 6–7 min

Watch carefully as we solve this equation using inverse operations. Notice every step!

🤔 Teacher asks: "What operations are happening to the variable x in this equation?"
📌 Step 1 — Undo Addition
3x + 10 = 28
3x + 10 − 10 = 28 − 10
3x = 18
"First we subtract 10 from both sides because +10 is happening to the x side. Subtraction undoes addition."
📌 Step 2 — Undo Multiplication
3x = 18
3x ÷ 3 = 18 ÷ 3
x = 6
"Now we divide both sides by 3 to undo the multiplication. The variable x is now isolated!"
✅ Step 3 — Check the Answer
3(6) + 10 = 28
18 + 10 = 28
28 = 28 ✓ Our solution is correct!
3

Guided Practice

⏱ 7–8 min

Now you help solve this equation with your teacher. Think through each step before looking at the answer!

🤔 "What should we undo first? What operation is happening to y?"
9y − 12 = 15solve together!
Step 1 — Undo Subtraction
9y − 12 + 12 = 15 + 12
9y = 27
"We added 12 to both sides because subtraction is happening. Addition is the inverse of subtraction."
🤔 "Why do we divide by 9 next? What is our solution?"
Step 2 — Undo Multiplication
9y ÷ 9 = 27 ÷ 9
y = 3
Check: 9(3) − 12 = 27 − 12 = 15 ✓
